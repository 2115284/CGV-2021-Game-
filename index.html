GAME OBJECTIVE

The world is under attack, zombies have taken over, the world is sick, the human race is ending. This virus developed in the labs by the government is spreading very rapidly and if nothing is done to stop the spreading, humans will be extinct in about, a month? or 2 weeks? or even less. Hey soldier, welcome to Shooter. The game objective is pretty much simple, kill the zombies before they attack you. Yes, that simple. This will reduce the spread of this virus, you get attacked, if you're lucky the virus will turn you into a zombie, otherwise you'll die ( both options are not nice are they? ). Here is how it goes, the zombies will come your way, whether you decide to run, hide or I don't know what, they will find you and they will kill you. The more you run, the more zombies there'll be and trust me you don't wanna deal with hundreds and hundres of zombies, so kill the specified number of zombies as you can as quickly as you possibly can under the specified time period. As time goes more zombies will show up. So do it quickly and save the human race.
